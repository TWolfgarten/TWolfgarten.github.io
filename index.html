Thomas Wolfgarten, I am currently a PhD student at IGPP, the Institute for Global Pandemic Planning, at the University of Warwick. Originally an Economist ( University of Cologne) and Statistician (University of Warwick), my research focusses on measuring and modelling human behaviour during pandemics, using large scale data sets, like mobile phone location data. My research is multi-disciplinary, using Mathematical Epidemiology and Behavioural Science. My supervisors are Matt Keeling (Math Epi) and Neil Stewart (Beh Sci). Besides IGPP I am also associated with Warwick's SBIDER group and WBS .
